Egg Fried Rice

Description

This is one of my favorite dishes, for one because it's so easy, but also because it is incredibly fast to make.

The best flavour comes from day old rice!

Ingredients

  • egg
  • fry
  • rice

Steps

  1. Make rice one day before
  2. Use wok and add yummy stuff for flavour
  3. Beat eggs, add salt, and stir among rice to cover each granule
  4. Fry.
  5. Enjoy!
